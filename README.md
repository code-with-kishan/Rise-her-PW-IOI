## 🌸 Overview

**RISEher** is a comprehensive, AI-driven digital empowerment ecosystem purpose-built for women entrepreneurs across India and beyond. It is not just a business tool — it is a complete platform that integrates artificial intelligence, financial management, personal safety, community networking, skill development, and an immersive 3D marketplace into a single, unified experience.

At its core, RISEher believes that every woman entrepreneur deserves equal access to intelligent tools, mentorship, financial resources, and a safe environment — both online and offline. The platform removes the traditional barriers to entrepreneurship by combining cutting-edge technology with a deep understanding of the real-world challenges faced by women in business.

> *"RISEher is more than a platform — it is a movement powered by AI, built for the women who dare to dream, build, and lead."*

---

## 🎯 Vision & Mission

### 🌺 Vision
To create a **safe, intelligent, and inclusive digital ecosystem** that enables women entrepreneurs to achieve financial independence, professional growth, and personal well-being through technology-driven solutions — making world-class entrepreneurial tools accessible to every woman, regardless of background or location.

### 🎌 Mission
RISEher bridges the gap between **entrepreneurship, education, safety, and technology** by providing women with a unified platform that supports every stage of their entrepreneurial journey — from ideation and launch to scale and global expansion.

## ✨ Full Feature Breakdown

### 🤖 AI Business Assistant — Your Personal Mentor

The RISEher AI Business Assistant is an intelligent, conversational mentor powered by large language models. It acts as a 24/7 business advisor that understands context, remembers your goals, and delivers personalized recommendations in real time.

- Generates complete **business plans** tailored to your industry, location, and goals
- Performs **market analysis** and competitor research to help you position your brand
- Provides **financial insights** including revenue forecasting, expense planning, and profit analysis
- Recommends **funding opportunities** — grants, angel investors, VCs, and accelerators — with eligibility matching
- Develops **marketing strategies** across digital, social, and offline channels
- Creates **growth roadmaps** broken into actionable quarterly milestones
- Offers **personalized coaching** based on your progress and business stage

---

### 🎤 Voice AI Assistant — Hands-Free Intelligence

- Full **English + Hindi** support with Auto-detect language mode
- **Speech-to-text** and **text-to-speech** for complete two-way voice interaction
- Commands auto-submit after you finish speaking — no button press needed
- Adjustable voice volume and language preferences saved per session
- Navigate any platform module, ask business questions, and get emergency support — all by voice

---

### 🧊 3D Product Visualization — Immersive Commerce

- AI converts 2D product photos into interactive **3D mesh models**
- **Three.js + WebGL** render models in-browser — no app download required
- Drag to rotate 360° · Scroll to zoom · Right-click to pan
- Increases purchase confidence, reduces return rates, and elevates brand presentation
- Every marketplace product tagged with a "3D Model" badge when a model is available

## 🚀 Getting Started

```bash
# Clone the repository
git clone https://github.com/code-with-kishan/Rise-her-PW-IOI.git

# Navigate to project
cd Rise-her-PW-IOI

# Install dependencies
npm install

# Set up environment variables
cp .env.example .env
# Add your Firebase and API credentials to .env

# Start development server
npm run dev

# Build for production
npm run build
```
